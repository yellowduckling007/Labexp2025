HTML <title> Tag

What it is and why it matters

The <title> tag defines the title of an HTML document.

This title is displayed in the browser's title bar or tab, and is important for SEO and accessibility.

Here’s the basic syntax:

<title>Your Page Title</title>

Example usage:


<head>
  <title>My Awesome Website</title>
</head>

When you open the webpage, “My Awesome Website” will appear on the browser tab or window title.
